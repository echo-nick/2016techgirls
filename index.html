I am a Web Developer at Echo & Co. This is my website! My job is to help take website designs and turn them into functioning websites that help nonprofits and other civic-minded organizations help get their message out and spread their missions across the world.

But did you know I wasn't always a developer? In fact, I did a bunch of stuff before I started making websites at Echo, including:

  • Writing web content for an SEO company
  • Helping patients get better at a physical therapy clinic
  • Taking care of people who were hurt or sick as an EMT with the fire department

Anyway, now I do what I do and I'm really excited about it. I've gotten to work on a number of cool websites for some great organizations, including the Newseum, Brightfocus, ioby and more.

Not only is the work we do at Echo pretty cool, but the office is cool too! We're located right in the heart of DC, a stone's throw away from the White House - that's the actual White House, where the President of the United States lives! It's a hub for nonprofits like the kind we love working with and definitely the spot to be.

In addition to our DC office, we have an office in Boston (well, Somerville), MA and New Orleans, LA. A few Dittos even work remotely most of the time. Even though we're all spread out, we work very well together. Our team is growing and seems to get stronger every day, so it's a great place to learn and grow!
